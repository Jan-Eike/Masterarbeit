Should surrogate motherhood be allowed?
Surrogacy brings parenthood to couples unable of have children of their own.
Surrogacy need not involve money payments of any kind.
Surrogacy is not inherently exploitative; exploitation can be minimized
Surrogate mothers could be a friend of the family to avoid payments
On a practical note, no law against surrogacy could be enforced properly.
Parenthood, through surrogacy, is not a fundamental right.
Surrogacy will inevitably become commercial.
Commercial surrogacy is exploitative.
Commercialize surrogacy will benefit the rich more than the poor.
It is a bad idea for surrogate mothers to be friends of the family.
The difficulty of enforcing surrogacy laws does not justify abandoning them.
